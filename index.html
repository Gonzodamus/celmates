CELMATES
Welcome to Celmates, a podcast where we compare, contrast, analyze and deconstruct two animated films. They're mostly Disney (but not always), mostly musicals (but not always), and mostly good (but not always). We're going to explore the works of Disney, Pixar, Don Bluth, Hayao Miyazaki, Dreamworks and more. We'll also dive into the depths of knockoff movies and the stuff that's just plain weird.
